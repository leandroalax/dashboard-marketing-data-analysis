# Data Visualization Rationale

---

This document explains the rationale behind the selection of each visualization used in the **Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard**, ensuring clarity, analytical coherence, and alignment with exploratory objectives.

---

## 1. Customer Overview

### KPI Cards

**Purpose:** Provide an immediate high-level summary of the dataset.

* Total Customers
* Average Annual Income
* Total Purchases by Channel (Store, Web, Catalog)
* Total Discount Purchases
* Etc.

<img width="1227" height="216" alt="print - kpi cards" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7970291b-7620-4977-b47b-dfcf2d338d4c" />


**Rationale:** KPI cards allow decision-makers to quickly grasp the scale of the customer base and the relevance of each purchase channel before diving into detailed analysis.

---

### Bar Charts – Education Level and Marital Status

<img width="1221" height="261" alt="print - bars grafic_educacion lvl e marital status " src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f691515e-443e-4324-bcb2-7cbc484ddfcc" />

**Purpose:** Understand customer distribution across key demographic dimensions.

## 2. Customer Purchase Behavior

### Scatter Plot – Total Spend vs. Annual Income

**Purpose:** Identify correlations between income level and customer spending.
